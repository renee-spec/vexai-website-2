# Pricing source of truth

Canonical pricing for Vexai lives at https://www.vexai.nl/prijzen/ .

The widget does NOT hardcode prices. Pricing is discussed in the kennismaking.

If the pricing page structure changes, update the widget's price-redirect line in `public/widget/vexai-widget.js` (search for "transparant over prijzen" in the COPY object).
